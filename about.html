about
tou café's mission: to let those with nut allergies feel seen.

Have you ever wanted to eat something that looks delicious, only to find out it was made with nuts in some way? Does your body ever hate nuts so much that it would rather die than consume one? If you answered yes to any of these questions, then you're in luck!


As someone with severe nut allergies, I am forced to pass up on mouth-watering food every single day of my life. Cookies? Has traces of nuts. Cake? Manufactured in a facility that processes nuts. A whole restaurant? Nope, there's cross-contamination with nuts.


Anyway, guess what? I got tired of it, so I manifested tou café (the lowercase makes it chill). The purpose of tou café is to keep track of recipes so you too can enjoy cool and yummy desserts (among other stuff).

the meaning of "SFK"
tou café is dedicated to being 100% nut-free*.

SFK is an acronym that stands for "Safe For Kyu" (Kyu being, well, me). Conversely, NSFK stands for "Not Safe For Kyu."


It's used to describe food I can eat without dying, so essentially it's a label for any food item that doesn't contain nuts. I think it started off as a joke I made with my friends, but then we ended up using it more and then it stuck.


*Some "-nuts" are special exceptions, like coconut.
