pumpkin muffin

  • (all-purpose) flour
  • (granulated) sugar
  • baking powder
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • pumpkin spice
  • pumpkin purée
  • (un?)salted butter
  • vanilla extract
  • eggs
  • cream cheese
  • milk
  • 2 small microwave-safe bowls
  • large bowl
  • medium bowl
  • muffin tin
  • piping bag/ziplock bag with one of its bottom corners cut off
  • oven at 350°f
  • wire rack
  • cupcake wrappers
  • big cool-looking plate

u can't miss out if ur baking it urself (coping)

melt 1/4 cup butter into a small microwave-safe bowl
  • butter can be unsalted or salted
  • to melt, cover the top with plastic wrap and microwave for 36 seconds, then in 9-second intervals until it's all/mostly liquid
lightly beat 1 large egg into another small bowl
mix 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour, 3/4 cup sugar, 1 tsp baking powder, 1 tsp baking soda, 1/4 tsp salt, and 1 tsp pumpkin spice into a large bowl until combined
mix 1 1/2 cup pumpkin purée, the butter, ~2 teaspoon vanilla paste/extract, and the egg into a medium bowl
make a crater in the middle of the large bowl and pour/spoon the contents of the medium bowl into the large bowl
mix the large bowl until just combined and there are no dry pockets
fill the 12-cup muffin pan evenly
wash the medium bowl
using a hand mixer on medium speed, mix 4 oz softened cream cheese, ~1 teaspoon vanilla bean paste/extract, 1 tsp all-purpose flour, 2 tbsp 2 tsp granulated sugar, and 1 tsp milk into the medium bowl until smooth
put mixture into either piping bag or ziplock bag
evenly fill the insides of each muffin with the cream (squeeze for ~1 second)
evenly top each muffin with the cream
preheat the oven to 350°f
bake for ~18 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the bread comes out mostly clean
let them cool in the tin for 10+ minutes
transfer to wire rack to cool completely (time varies)
fit each muffin with a cupcake wrapper
transfer to a cool-looking plate big enough to hold the muffins
return home
