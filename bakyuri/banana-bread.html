banana bread

ingredients
  • bananas
    • the more bananas the better (3-4 is the sweet spot)
    • the riper the banana the better (75%+ brown is good)
    • the longer the banana the better (6+ inches works)
  • butter
    • butter can be salted or unsalted
    • to melt, cut into smaller pieces, put into a small microwave-safe bowl, cover the top with plastic wrap, microwave for 27 seconds, and then in 9-second intervals until it's all/mostly liquid
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • (granulated) sugar
  • large eggs
  • vanilla extract
  • (all-purpose) flour
  • chocolate chips (any kind)
    • optional
    • white or semi-sweet are the best kinds
equipment
  • small microwave-safe bowl
  • medium bowl
  • large fork
  • spatula
  • 9x5 inch loaf pan
  • oven at 350°f
  • big cool-looking plate
instructions
melt 1/3 cup butter in a small microwave-safe bowl
using a huge fork, mash 2+ ripe bananas into a medium-sized bowl
mix the butter into the bananas
mix in 1/2 teaspoon baking soda and 1 pinch (1/16 teaspoon) salt
beat 1 large egg in the bowl used for the butter
mix in 3/4 cup (granulated) sugar, the beaten egg, and ~2 teaspoon vanilla extract
mix in 1 1/2 cup (all-purpose) flour until everything's just been mixed in
mix in ~1/3 cup chocolate chips
preheat the oven to 350°f
butter all sides of a 9x5 inch loaf pan (with butter or cooking spray)
transfer banana bread mixture to the loaf pan
add ~1/4 cup chocolate chips on the top and spread them around
bake for ~55 minutes or until a toothpick stabbed into the bread comes out clean
transfer to a cool-looking plate big enough to hold the banana bread
