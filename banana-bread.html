banana bread

  • ripe bananas
  • (un?)salted butter
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • (granulated) sugar
  • large eggs
  • vanilla extract
  • (all-purpose) flour
  • chocolate chips (any kind)
  • butter or cooking spray
  • small microwave-safe bowl
  • medium-sized bowl
  • huge fork
  • spatula
  • 9x5 inch loaf pan
  • oven at 350°f
  • big cool-looking plate

this banana bread is very pog everyone likes it

melt 1/3 cup (5 1/3 tbsp) butter into a small microwafe-safe bowl
  • butter can be unsalted or salted
  • to melt, cover the top with plastic wrap and microwave for 36 seconds, then in 9-second intervals until it's all/mostly liquid
using a huge fork, mash 2+ ripe bananas into a medium-sized bowl
  • the more bananas the better (3-4 is the sweet spot)
  • the riper the banana the better (75%+ brown is good)
  • standard banana size is 6-8 inches
mix the butter into bananas
beat 1 large egg
  • do it in the bowl used for the butter
mix in 1/2 tsp baking soda and 1 pinch (1/16 tsp) salt
mix in 3/4 cup (granulated) sugar, the beaten egg, and ~2 tsp vanilla extract
mix in 1 1/2 cup flour until everything's just been mixed
mix in chocolate chips until it looks like there's enough
  • any kind of chocolate works; semi-sweet or white are the best ones
preheat the oven to 350°f
butter all sides of a 9x5 inch loaf pan (with actual butter or with cooking spray)
transfer to the pan
add some more chocolate chips on the top and spread them around
bake for ~55 minutes or until a toothpick stabbed into the bread comes out clean
transfer to a cool-looking plate big enough to hold the banana bread
return home
